\section{Figures}

All figures in this thesis were generated from experimental data collected during
controlled collision tests at the ITU REALLAB. Trajectory plots, IMU traces, and
comparative performance diagrams were produced using a custom Python analysis
pipeline and are included as raster or vector graphics as indicated in their captions.

Vector diagrams (\texttt{.pdf}) were authored in Mermaid (\texttt{.mmd}) and rendered
using the headless Mermaid CLI, ensuring consistent scaling and resolution in the
final document.
